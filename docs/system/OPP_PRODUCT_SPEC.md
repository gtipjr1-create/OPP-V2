# OPP Product Spec

## What OPP Is
OPP is a Personal Command Center for disciplined living and focused building. It is a personal operating system that reduces noise and keeps direction, standards, and execution aligned.

## What OPP Is Not
- Not a generic productivity app
- Not a habit-tracker clone
- Not a social app
- Not a feature-sprawl dashboard
- Not a casual notes bucket

## Core Screens
- Today / Command Desk:
  Re-entry and orientation surface for what matters now.
- Domains:
  Structural life areas with deliberate emphasis states.
- Priorities (Focus):
  Active commitment-management layer.
- Standards:
  Governing rules and non-negotiables.
- Weekly Anchors / This Week:
  Short-horizon directional commitments.

## v1 Purpose
Deliver a calm, low-noise operating layer with strong conceptual boundaries so daily action, focus commitments, and governing standards do not collapse into one another.

## Parked-State Status
OPP is parked, not abandoned. The current goal is continuity and preservation of model clarity, not expansion. Resume work should begin with verification and boundary checks before any feature development.

## Weekly Anchors Role

Weekly Anchors define short-horizon direction.

They are:
- not tasks
- not commitments in the same sense as Priorities
- not long-term goals

They act as:
- directional constraints for the current week
- alignment layer between Domains and Today

They should remain:
- limited in number
- stable across the week
- clearly tied to active domains

## Today Rules

Today is the execution surface.

It should:
- clearly show what matters now
- remain limited in scope
- avoid becoming a backlog
- reflect active priorities and weekly anchors

It should not:
- become a dumping ground
- duplicate Priorities
- hold long-term items
